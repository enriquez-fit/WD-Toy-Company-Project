Bridge

Body Type: Normal

Pose: Bridge

Found: Around corners of the room/p>

Always deep in thought. To think flexibly, one must act flexibly! ..which is the reason for this pose.

Peeking

Body Type: Slim

Pose: Peeking down

Found: On top of objects and shelves

Finds comfort in high places and likes to look down at the scenery. Weary of things and when it notices something it makes sure to check it out first from a safe distance.

Climbing

Body Type: Chubby

Pose: Rope Climbing

Found: Around ropes, strings, cords

Curious of what is waiting at the top of the hanging string. Will climb up without hesitation but always has some trouble getting back down. Loves adventure.

Little

Body Type: Normal

Pose: One hand against the wall

Found: Near walls

Little Smiski have made their appearance! For some reason these little ones like to line up against the wall in this pose.

Hiding

Body Type: Normal

Pose: Hiding under something

Found: Underneath towels and blankets

Likes to be in small, cosy places. Found underneath towels and blankets, looking around its surroundings while hiding. Is a little timid in personality.

Handstand

Body Type: Chubby

Pose: Handstand

Found: Around corners of the room

Always found doing a handstand against the wall. If you look closely, the peeking Smiski from Series 2 is actually always trying to copy this Smiski.
